FREE Learning Language

Freell, pronounced 'for real,' lets AI decode learning rules without error. It helps educators turn key ideas into software. The goal is to cut unnecessary constraints in education design, giving learners more freedom.

LEarnGAme

Lega combines playful gaming with learning to learn. While playing for fun, you unconsciously improve skills to design your own effective learning process. These design skills grant you the freedom to explore and navigate your learning journey more liberally.

EDucation ARchitect

Edar is a tool for education architects to efficiently design, manage, and optimize free learning programs. It simplifies the workflow, letting professionals focus on creativity and expertise to enhance freedom in learning.

EDucator's DAshboard

EDDA provides detailed insights into individual and group learning paths, enhancing learner freedom. It enables educators to confidently let students steer their own learning, knowing they can effectively monitor and intervene when needed.
